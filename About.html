Who We Are

We’re a family-run business on a mission to make healthier food and drink choices accessible through high-quality vending machines. Built by a father-son duo, our company is dedicated to changing what vending means to the modern customer.

Our Mission

To provide convenient, nutritious snacks and beverages in safe, high-traffic locations while supporting local wellness initiatives.

Our Story

Founded on the idea of combining business with purpose, Healthy Vending Solutions was created to bring more than just snacks to schools, gyms, hospitals, and beyond—we bring value, health, and peace of mind.
